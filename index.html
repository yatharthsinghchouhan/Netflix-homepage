Popular Movies and TV Shows

3 ediots

3 Idiots" is a popular Bollywood comedy-drama film released in 2009. Directed by Rajkumar Hirani and produced by Vidhu Vinod Chopra, the movie is loosely based on Chetan Bhagat's novel "Five Point Someone." "3 Idiots" explores themes of education, friendship, and the pursuit of one's true passions.

Chichore

Chhichhore" is a Hindi-language Indian film released in 2019. The film was directed by Nitesh Tiwari and produced by Sajid Nadiadwala. It is known for its blend of comedy and drama and deals with themes of friendship, nostalgia, and the pressures faced by students in the Indian education system.

DDLJ

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge," often abbreviated as DDLJ, is a classic Bollywood romantic film released in 1995. It is considered one of the most iconic and beloved movies in Indian cinema. The film was directed by Aditya Chopra and produced by Yash Chopra.
